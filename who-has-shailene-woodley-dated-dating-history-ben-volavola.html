A Look Inside Shailene Woodley's Dating History

Shailene Woodley's dating life has been in the headlines far more than usual lately, as fans follow along with all the gossip and updates surrounding her relationship with Aaron Rodgers. This celebrity couple has given fans all the feels, but it has also put more spotlight on Woodley's past dating life, as well.Since she has been in the spotlight for this relationship, fans have started to dig deeper into what her dating life was like before Aaron came along, and it turns out that she's been linked to some very prominent men. She has been in some low-key relationships and has also been in some that have received a lot more media attention. Here's what her dating history has been like for the past several years...

8 Shailene Woodley And Elliot Page

The rumor mill ran wild in 2014 when Shailene Woodley and Elliot Page were spotted together and were assumed to be dating. It was no secret that they were longtime friends, but things took a turn at one point, and the headlines started to suggest that they had stepped outside their friendship and had gone on to explore a romantic relationship, too. There are more than a few images of these two sharing moments with one another yet neither has come forward to firmly confirm or deny the dating rumors, leaving fans to wonder what actually happened between them.

7 Shailene Woodley And Nahko Bear

Shailene Woodley and Nahko Bear were linked for two solid years, from 2014-2016, and they seemed to be inseparable and happily involved in their romance. Nahko Bear's Indie rock roots caught Woodley's attention, and before long the two were jet-setting to Hawaii to enjoy a romantic vacation together. Even after their flame had faded, Shailene and Nahko Bear remained good friends, and she continued to promote his music, showing he still had her full support as a friend.

6 Shailene Woodley And Ezra Miller

Shailene Woodley and famed actor and singer Ezra Miller shared a romantic relationship in February 2016, and while they were together, they made some red carpet appearances and lived it up in front of the cameras. They seem to have only hooked up for a short while but were visibly happy in one another's company during that time.

5 Shailene Woodley And Theo James

Fans want to know more about what really transpired between Shailene Woodley and Theo James. The Divergent stars spent a lot of time together in 2016, and many believed them to be dating at that time. Making matters even more interesting was the fact that Theo claimed he had "instant chemistry" with Shailene, which many believed to be a romantic reference.

4 Shailene Woodley And Ben Volavola

Ben Volavola and Shailene Woodley made their relationship Instagram official in January 2018. This was after they had already sparked romantic rumors the year before. Fans thought this would be the relationship that would stick, but it was soon revealed that they had broken up, with Woodley claiming "she was not ready for the next step." When speaking about this breakup, Shailene said, “I realized I was still at an age where I wasn’t able to fully commit. I couldn’t be available to him in the way that I wanted to be. I didn’t fully love myself.”'

3 Shailene And Aaron Rodgers Start Dating

Shailene Woodley and Aaron Rodgers stunned fans in February 2021 when they confirmed they were dating. Upon sharing the big news, fans instantly wanted to know more. Shailene interestingly revealed that she knows nothing at all about football, and had no scope or understanding about Aaron's success in the NFL.

She went on to describe that the two had started off as friends and then became involved in long-distance dating. They both seemed smitten with one another, and fans were excited to see where this would lead. Nobody had any idea that huge news was just around the bend...

2 Aaron Rodgers And Shailene Woodley's Sudden Engagement

When accepting an award at the NFL Honors Awards ceremony, Aaron Rodgers thanked his "fiancée" for her support, and the headlines instantly erupted. This was the first anyone had heard that Shailene and Aaron were officially engaged, and the questions began flooding in.

It turns out that things really heated up for the couple after having spent much of their time together in lockdown. The engagement news came as a shock and evoked some mixed reactions from fans. Some thought this to be a beautiful next step for the couple, while others believed that Shailene and Aaron were moving far too fast.

1 Shailene And Aaron Rodgers' Break-Up And Possible Make-Up

On February 16th, 2022, roughly a year after their engagement, it was revealed that Shailene Woodley and Aaron Rodgers had called off their big plans. The couple reached a mutual agreement to split up and go their separate ways. Just as quickly as they had called it quits, the two were spotted together again. On March 9th, the former couple were spotted arm-in-arm in South Florida, and reports indicated they had attended a wedding together... as a couple. What this means for their future remains unknown.
