Chris Brown's Ex-Girlfriend Reacts To Criticism Of Her New Foot Photo Business

There was a time when Karrueche Tran was in the media because of her relationship with her ex-boyfriend, Chris Brown, but now her business ventures are attracting attention. Tran has an OnlyFans focused on posting content of her feet, which has created a reaction among fans and critics. She is aware of the chatter surrounding her decision, and has responded with a since-deleted post on X (formerly Twitter).

What Does Karrueche Tran Do For Work?

Tran's focus on OnlyFans has also provided jobs. "I have a team that helps with the page, so they'll be able to eat from this as well," she wrote on X. "There's a stigma around OF, but there's literally chefs, musicians, yoga instructors, etc. on there." But this is not her only business, and Tran has worked hard to establish herself and her career. This includes working as an actress on the drama series Claws, and being signed to Wilhelmina Models.

Before embracing a career in the entertainment industry, Tran was a personal shopper, and she discussed this in an interview with W magazine in 2017. "It's influenced me to keep working hard," she said of how her job in fashion influenced her. "To go from where I started in a department store to acting is such a big leap for me. It motivates me to push myself harder and succeed, to understand that life is unexpected, and to be ready to handle whatever comes your way. I never expected to become an actor at all."

What Has Karrueche Tran Said About Her Relationship With Chris Brown?

Tran and Brown started dating in 2011, and their tumultuous relationship lasted four years. Tran ultimately ended their relationship because of cheating allegations and the "Run It!" singer fathering a child. In 2021, Tran addressed why this relationship was not good for her during an appearance on The Angie Martinez Show. "I was trying to find my way and trying to make it through somehow, one day at a time," she said. "And now, looking back, I'm like, 'Girl, you were going through it.' Even I can just tell from the way I looked and just everything, I was a different girl."
