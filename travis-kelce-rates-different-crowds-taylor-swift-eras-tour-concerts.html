Travis Kelce Rates Different Crowds From Taylor Swift's Eras Tour Concerts

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce has been joining Taylor Swift on tour to support her and her fans.
  • He found the crowds in Singapore more interesting than in Australia, but enjoyed both locations despite limited time spent there.
  • Now that their busy tour schedules are on hold, Taylor and Travis are focused on relaxing together at home in Los Angeles.

Now that football season is over and the Eras tour stopping for a few months, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have been making the most of their downtime. Ever since their relationship started they have been incredibly busy, and while they made the most of it and made sure to spend as much time together as they could, they now have the opportunity to be a normal couple.

Travis Kelce joined Taylor Swift on a few dates of the singer's tour, and has gotten to know her fans and the way they react to the incredible show that she performs in her three-hour-long concerts. When talking about the shows he attended, he had this to say about Taylor's audiences.

Travis Kelce About The Crowds From Taylor Swift's Concerts

Travis Kelce has now attended several concerts to support Taylor Swift, and since her Eras tour has taken her all over the world, he has gotten to know many different crowds. The most recent, and the last for a while, was the Singapore leg of her tour, and now that the couple are entering a quiet period of time as they slow down the pace of their busy careers, he reflected on the time he spent with her around the world.

When his brother Jason Kelce asked him to compare the Singapore and Australian audiences, he had to take a moment to think about it. "Man that's a tough sell, I loved them both," he said. "I want to see more of Australia, I feel like I got to see a little bit more of Singapore. So I'm going to say Singapore was a little bit more interesting for sure."

It's important to point out that he was only in Australia for about a day, so he didn't get to know the people that much. "I feel like Sydney is just like Tampa, I don't think it's that much different," he added. "Which is probably why I want to see more of it, I'm intrigued, I'm there. The Aussies know how to have a good time man."

Going On Tour Was Fun, But Taylor And Travis Are Ready To Spend Some Quiet Time Together

If there's something that Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have proven it's that they are willing to go above and beyond to support each other. They have both, in separate instances, literally traveled halfway across the world to attend important moments in the other's career, and while that's fun and a testament to their commitment to each other, it's obviously tiring.

According to new reports, the couple are now "nesting" in Taylor's home in Los Angeles, enjoying some of their well-deserved time off.

"They’re focused on rest and recuperation and relaxing at home and having low-key and chill time together," a source shared. "They are enjoying movie nights in her home theater, catching up on films and shows they’ve missed."

Although they are both obviously very career-oriented and love what they do, at this point in their lives and relationship they were looking forward to "a break from the buzz of their careers."

"They can finally rest and intend on spending quality time together and with friends and family," the source continued, and as they get more serious, they will schedule their projects in the future in a way that allows them to "see each other as much as possible."

Swifties will have to wait a while to get more of Travis' reviews of Taylor's crowds, but if things continue this way, he will be present at a lot more concerts when the pop star goes on the road again.
