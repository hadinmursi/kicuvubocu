Alyson Hannigan Bikini Body: See Picture of Star in Hawaii

It isn't a stretch: Alyson Hannigan looks great in a bikini! The last time the How I Met Your Mother donned a two-piece (on Sept. 19 to be exact), she was photographed stretching with her arms over her head, which made her ribs stick out, sparking much discussion online about whether she was too skinny.

New photos, taken on Monday, Sept. 30 in Hawaii, prove the 39-year-old actress is healthy and in perfect shape. The mom of two girls showed off her washboard abs and slim figure in a pink and purple print two-piece as she played in the water with her 4-year-old Satyana and actor husband Alexis Denisof, 47.
