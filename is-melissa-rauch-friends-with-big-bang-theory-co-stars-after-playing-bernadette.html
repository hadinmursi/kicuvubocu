Is Melissa Rauch Still Friends With Her Co-Stars After Playing Bernadette On The Big Bang Theory?

Highlights

  • Melissa Rauch and Mayim Bialik have maintained a close friendship off-screen.
  • The cast of The Big Bang Theory always talks about staying in touch after the show ended.
  • Melissa Rauch and her co-stars have expressed love and support for each other's projects.

The Big Bang Theory star Melissa Rauch probably never has to work again after playing Bernadette Rostenkowski on the popular sitcom. Of course, the actress is so talented that she has continued to take on roles since the show went off the air in 2019. Even though TBBT fans were split on Bernadette, there's no question that Rauch became an integral member of the cast.

Since Melissa Rauch has always been positive about her days on The Big Bang Theory set, fans hope that she has maintained relationships with her co-stars since the series finale. There's nothing more upsetting and disappointing than learning that a TV cast secretly dislikes each other. Here's the truth about how friendly Melissa Rauch is with the other The Big Bang Theory actors.

Is The Big Bang Theory Star Melissa Rauch Friends With Mayim Bialik Today?

Since Melissa Rauch and Mayim Bialik were cast on The Big Bang Theory later than their co-stars, fans are often curious about whether they are friends.

Viewers first see Rauch and Bialik's characters Bernadette Rostenkowski and Amy Farrah Fowler in season 3 of the sitcom. Bernadette and Amy get to know each other since they are dating Howard Wolowitz and Sheldon Cooper. But did the actors bond off-screen?

It definitely seems like the actors have always been close to each other. In general, The Big Bang Theory seemed to have a friendly and positive cast who loved being together on set. Even back in 2016, when Rauch starred in the movie The Bronze, Bialik interviewed her about the project for the website Kveller.

In April 2019, when the sitcom was ending, Bilak shared a beautiful message about Rauch on her Instagram account. She summed up the good times they had shared together and it sounded like she really cared about her costar.

She wrote, "This lady and I have been through a lot together as the new kids on the block of season 3 of [Big Bang Theory]. We have cried together and laughed together and raised kids on a set together. I couldn't have made it without her with any semblance of sanity. Thank you [themelissarauch] for being you. You're so much more than [thebronzemovie] to me. You're solid gold."

Whenever the actors are asked about their castmates, they always talk about trying their best to remain close. In August 2020, Mayim Bialik talked about The Big Bang Theory cast in an interview with Us Weekly, and Rauch was one of the actors that she mentioned.

Bialik said, “Melissa [Rauch] [and] I [have] talked to Simon [Helberg]. I actually wrote a screenplay and I’ve been wanting to cast him in this screenplay, so I was able to do that. I don’t know if we’ll get to make the movie ‘cause quarantine.” She also mentioned that Jim Parsons was a producer on her sitcom Call Me Kat, which enabled them to stay in contact with each other.

In a 2020 interview with Fox News, Bialik said "I talk to Melissa most often." It's really nice to hear that the two actors are still so special to each other.

Is Melissa Rauch Still Friendly With The Rest Of The Big Bang Theory Cast?

Melissa Rauch has always shared how much she loves her co-stars. In 2016, she told Smashing Interviews, "I love the TV family that I am so lucky to be a part of. It is such a wonderful group of people whom I adore being around."

In the years after she played Bernadette on The Big Bang Theory, there has never been any indication that Melissa Rauch has stopped being friends with her castmates. In fact, they seem to care about each other as much as they ever did.

Melissa Rauch's more recent interviews tend to focus on her role in Night Court, which makes sense since it sounds like she truly loves this project. She doesn't talk about her friendships all that much.

However, when many of her castmates talk to the media, they often mention that they are all still great friends.

In March 2021, Kaley Cuoco talked about her co-stars in an interview with Metro.co.uk and said that at that point in time, they were texting one another regularly.

While of course she was totally right, it's still bittersweet to think about the show ending. The cast was used to seeing each other all the time and so this must have been a huge adjustment.

The Big Bang Theory actors must feel good that even though they aren't on set together all the time anymore, they always prove that they still matter to each other. It's hard to imagine Melissa Rauch and her co-stars ever fighting or deciding that they don't want to be friends anymore. They shared something special and it doesn't sound like they will ever forget the experience of starring in The Big Bang Theory together.
