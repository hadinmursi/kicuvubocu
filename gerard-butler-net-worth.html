Gerard Butler Net Worth

Gerard Butler is a wealthy Scottish actor with an estimated net worth of $80 million. He is perhaps best known today for his role as Spartan King Leonidas in the film “300.”

This performance earned the actor numerous awards and accolades. Gerard later became well-known for his starring role in the film “Olympus Has Fallen” and its spin-offs. In addition to these achievements, Butler is a well-known voice actor and producer.

Gerard Butler was reported to have purchased a home in Glasgow, Scotland, in 2015. He paid 582,000 pounds for the home, which has three bedrooms and a private balcony.

A year later, in 2016, it was revealed that Butler had listed his Los Feliz home for $4.4 million. Butler purchased the home in 2008 for $3.25 million, and he was also renting it out at the time for $15,000 per month.

Gerard Butler also owns a 3,300-square-foot duplex loft in Manhattan’s Chelsea neighbourhood, which he purchased in 2004 for $2.57 million.

Gerard was reported to have spent $65,000 on the initial reconstruction process after his home was severely damaged by fires in California in 2019. Butler admitted that the Malibu home had been partially destroyed.

He paid $6.45 million for the mansion, which sits on 1.67 acres of land. Butler was one of many celebrities whose homes were destroyed during this time period.
