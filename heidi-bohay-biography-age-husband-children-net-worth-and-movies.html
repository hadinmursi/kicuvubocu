Heidi Bohay Biography, Age, Husband, Children, Net Worth and Movies

Heidi Bohay Biography

Heidi Bohay is an American actress and television presenter born on December 15, 1959 in New Jersey, United States. She is most best known for her role as Megan Kendall from 1983 to 1987 on the prime-time ABC drama Hotel.

She attended Bound Brook High School in New Jersey where she graduated in 1977. She appeared as a celebrity guest on game shows, including The $25,000 and $100,000 Pyramid, All-Star Blitz, New Liar’s Club and Hollywood Squares.

Heidi was the hostess of the cable television shows American Baby and Party at Home and she also co-hosted the interactive TV show GSN Live on GSN from the show’s debut on February 25, 2008.

Heidi Bohay Age

Heidi Bohay was born on December 15, 1959 in South Bound Brook, New Jersey, United States. She is 58 years old as of 2018.

Heidi Bohay Husband – Heidi Bohay Children

She has been married to Michael Spound since May 29, 1988 whom she met her husband, Michael Spound, when the two of them were cast members of the television drama, Hotel (1983-1988). The couple has has three sons; Zachary Spound (born 26 June 1989), Jonah Spound and Gabriel Spound.
