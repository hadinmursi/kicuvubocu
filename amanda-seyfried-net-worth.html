How Much Is Actress Amanda Seyfried Worth?

Back in the '90s, Amanda Seyfried was a staple on all kinds of TV shows, soap operas, and movies. Of course, she was most memorable, in fans' estimations, in the film 'Mean Girls,' a legacy that still follows the actress today.

But unlike many other actresses from her era, Amanda has seen continued success ever since 1999, when she earned a role on 'As the World Turns.' The true test, of course, of Seyfried's staying power is essentially, how much is she worth today?

While Seyfried has had plenty of lucrative roles, she's also passed on a few potentially career-propelling opportunities, including one gig on 'Guardians of the Galaxy.' That makes it sound like she's living pretty comfortably on her current earnings, doesn't it?

RELATED: Which MCU Character Did Amanda Seyfried Turn Down?

The fact is, Amanda probably doesn't need the money from the MCU. Her resume is pretty long without it, she's still a household name despite 'Mean Girls' premiering more than 15 years ago, and she's got no shortage of upcoming projects.

Two of her films are currently in post-production, and Seyfried also had three film credits in 2020 alone. It's not surprising, though; the actress has been working hard, and raking in the dough.

She's worth at least $12 million, says Celebrity Net Worth, which is definitely a respectable amount for a '90s star who's somehow managed to remain relevant as the Hollywood landscape has changed.

She has evolved a lot with the times, too. Starting out on a '90s soap opera before moving on to film and then back to TV for numerous appearances, Seyfried is clearly not a one-hit wonder. The fact that she was in demand enough to get an MCU offer probably tells half of the story.

But the actress also has a good head on her shoulders. She skipped the chance to attend college in New York City, says Celebrity Net Worth, to act in 'Mean Girls' instead. That intuition made her bank, and follow-ups to that hit earned millions at the box office. Though there's one film she's eager to re-do, if the opportunity presents itself, she's had a diverse journey in Hollywood.

Seyfried's resume also includes movies like 'Mamma Mia!' and its sequel, a film with Channing Tatum ('Dear John'), and a co-starring role with Justin Timberlake ('In Time').

She's put the cash to good use, too. Celebrity Net Worth points out that Amanda spent $1.85 on a mansion previously owned by Adam Brody (her 'Jennifer's Body' co-star), and she also owned a condo in NYC that she bought in 2010 for $1.7 million.

She seems pretty smart about money, and with continued movie projects adding even more length to her resume, Amanda can probably expect her net worth to rise, too.
