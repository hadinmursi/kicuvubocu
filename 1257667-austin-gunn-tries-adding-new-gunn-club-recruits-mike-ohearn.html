Austin Gunn Tries Adding New Gunn Club Recruits, Mike OHearn Tells Them To Suck It

The Gunn Club’s ranks will not include a Titan…

Austin Gunn shared the following video on social media, revealing he tried to get Mike O’Hearn to join the Gunn Club. O’Hearn, a seven-time Fitness Model of the Year and former American Gladiator, wasn’t having any of it, and forcefully throwing Gunn in a pool before telling the group they could all ‘suck it.’

The Gunn Club will probably retaliate but we’ll have to wait and see which arena can contain this fight. Will it be contested inside an AEW ring, or will we finally see the return of Battle Dome?

As if pissing off O’Hearn wasn’t enough, the Gunn Club recently started a feud with Paul Wight on AEW TV after Billy Gunn smacked him with a chair last month. Wight initially tried to make sense of the attack, but Gunn hit him a second time as Excalibur pointed out that Billy’s ‘scumbag sons,’ Austin and Colten, were just looking on as it happened.

Both Austin and Colten later commented on the incident, with Austin claiming it was “nothing personal” against Paul. Colten later took issue with Excalibur’s remark and said they were just making a statement, and he really meant “Sons of a Gunn.”
