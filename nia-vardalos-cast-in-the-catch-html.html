Nia Vardalos Has Been Cast in The Catch in a Role Probably Not at All Big, Fat, or Greek

Nia Vardalos.

You caught her in My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2, and now you can catch her in ABC’s The Catch. Ha. Nia Vardalos has been cast in the new Shonda Rhimes thriller drama (“thrama”), and according to Deadline, she’ll play “Leah Wells, a neurotic but formidable counterfeiter.” Classic Leah! The show will reunite Rhimes and Vardalos, and it will probably feel so good. (If you’ve been keeping up with Grey’s Anatomy, which might be on season 47 at this point — there’s no way to really know — Vardalos guest-starred.)
