Fisher Stevens Wife: Meet Alexis Bloom

Alexis Bloom is a film producer and the wife of film documentary producer Fisher Stevens.

Bloom is known for the films “Divide and Conquer (2018), We Steal Secrets (2013) and Bright Lights (2016).”

Bloom and Stevens got married in 2017, and together they have two children.

Who Is Fisher Stevens?

Fisher Stevens is an actor, producer, and director, famous for appearing in the 1986 American science fiction comedy Short Circuit and its sequel Short Circuit 2.

Born on November 27, 1963, in Chicago, Illinois, Stevens began his acting career in the 1980s and has since appeared in several film and TV projects.

After appearing on “Short Circuit” and “Short Circuit 2”, Stevens portrayed the character of Vince Latello in “My Science Project” (1985), and starred in many other films like “Hackers”, “The Flamingo Kid”, “The Grand Budapest Hotel”, The French Dispatch, Sing 2, Asteroid City, and many more.

On television, Stevens made appearances on “Lost”, “Damages”, “Elementary”, “Succession”, “The Blacklist”, “The Good Fight”, “Would I Lie To You?”, and several others.

As a producer cum director, Stevens is credited for the films “The Cove”, which won him an Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature; “Before the Flood”, “As We Go Green”, “Palmer”, and a few other productions.

Amongst a handful of awards in his name, Stevens is an Academy Award winner and a five-time Primetime Emmy Award nominee.
