How Did Ayesha Curry And Steph Meet?

Food Network host and actress, Ayesha Curry, had met NBA player Stephen Curry in a church youth group in Charlotte when they were just teenagers.

While Curry was pursuing her TV career in Hollywood and Stephen was playing college basketball at Davidson College, they soon began dating.

They eventually tied the knots on 2011 and are blessed with three children.

The couple and their children currently live in Atherton, California.

Ayesha Curry TV Career

Curry has graced the screens of several television shows before launching her own show, Ayesha’s Homemade (a.k.a. Ayesha’s Home Kitchen), on Food Network, in 2014.

Curry began with YouTube videos on her channel Little Lights of Mine and since then has garnered a huge fan base.

Her cookbook, The Seasoned Life, was published in 2016.

Curry has been a host on The Great American Baking Show, and even went ahead to collaborate with famous chef Michael Mina in The Mina Test Kitchen of International Smoke, a pop-up restaurant in the Bay Area.

Curry launched her Homemade the Oakland-based meal-kit delivery service, in 2017 and two years later, it became a full fledged retail store.
