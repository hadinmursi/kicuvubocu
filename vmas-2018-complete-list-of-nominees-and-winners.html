VMAs 2018: Complete List of Nominees and Winners

Updated at 11:52 p.m. ET

Camila Cabello! Cardi! J. Lo! MTV celebrated the biggest names in music at the 2018 Video Music Awards.

Rapper Cardi B led the pack with 10 nominations, while married couple Beyoncé and Jay-Z, nominated under their last name, The Carters, scored eight noms. Childish Gambino (real name Donald Glover) and Drake both had seven opportunities to take home a Moon Person statue, while Bruno Mars and Ariana Grande were not far behind with six and five nominations, respectively.
