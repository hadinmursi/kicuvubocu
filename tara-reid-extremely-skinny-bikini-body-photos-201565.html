Tara Reid Flashes Her Extremely Skinny Body in String Bikinis: Photos

Down Mexico way!

Tara Reid jetted to Cabo San Lucas for a little R&R with her friends, and on Tuesday, May 5, the actress shared a series of snaps from her vacation, which featured her extremely slender body in two printed string bikinis.

Per her mini vacation photo diary, Reid, 39, celebrated Cinco de Mayo in style, splashing in the ocean, lounging in hammocks, and snacking on "the best tacos [I've] ever had!" she wrote via Instagram.
