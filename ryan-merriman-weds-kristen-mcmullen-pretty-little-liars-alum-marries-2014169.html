Ryan Merriman Weds Kristen McMullen: Pretty Little Liars Alum Marries

They do! Pretty Little Liars alum Ryan Merriman wed his fiancee Kristen McMullen over the weekend of Sept. 5, his rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

 

Merriman, who played Torrey DeVitto's character Melissa Hastings' husband Ian Thomas on the ABC series until 2012, popped the question to McMullen in New Zealand back in April 2013.

"There is this awesome thing called Sky City, with a Stratosphere tower, and they light off about $300,000 worth of fireworks at midnight," the 42 actor previously explained to Us. "As soon as I gave her a kiss and proposed, the fireworks started going off. It was kind of like a movie."
