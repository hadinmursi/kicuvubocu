Chris Hemsworth Wife, Age, Children, Brother, Family, Height, Net Worth

Chris Hemsworth is a name that needs no introduction. He is one of the biggest stars Hollywood has produced in recent times, appearing in several blockbuster films such as Star Trek , Snow White and the Huntsman , Red Dawn , Onslaught, Vacation , In the Heart of the Sea , Men in Black: International, A Perfect Getaway , The Cabin in the Woods, and more.

However, Hemsworth is most popular for his role as Thor, the hammer-wielding god of thunder, in the Marvel cinematic universe. He first played the role in the 2011 film, Thor and has reprized the role in many other films such as The Avengers (2012), Thor: The Dark World (2013), Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015), Doctor Strange (2016) , Thor: Ragnarok (2017) and Avengers: Infinity War (2018 ) .

The following are facts on some of the things people have always wanted to know about this Chris.

Chris Hemsworth Bio (Age)

Christopher Hemsworth was born on August 11, 1983 in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. He is an Australian national of diverse ethnicity with German, Scottish, Dutch, English and Irish ancestry. Hemsworth spent his formative years in Melbourne as well as in a remote part of Australia known as The Outback. For his college education he attended Heathmonth College.

Chris Hemsworth began his film career in his native Australia, where he appeared in various television series. They include Guinevere Jones (2002), Neighbors (2002) and Marshall (2002). He got his first big break in 2004 when he landed the role of sexy but troubled Kim Hyde on the long-running Australian soap. home and away . The soap had previously served as a launch pad for the careers of other Australian actors and Hemsworth was no different. After a three-year stint on the show, he left for Hollywood in 2007.

It was in Tinseltown that he landed roles in films such as Star Trek (2009) and The Perfect Getaway (2010). He finally joined the big leagues in 2010 when he landed the popular role of Thor. He didn’t even expect to land the role due to a bad audition. In fact, his name hasn’t made the list of the last four players in the race. It was someone urging director Ken Branagh to give him another chance. Branagh did, and the rest, as they say, is history. Hemsworth has become one of Hollywood’s biggest stars. He has also won several awards and recognitions.

His capital

Chris Hemsworth has a net worth of between $70-$90 million. In 2018 alone, he earned $64.5 million, according to Forbes. His wealth comes from his successful film career as well as hefty endorsement deals. Hemsworth has certainly come a long way from a humble childhood in which he had to save up for months to afford a surfboard. Among his many possessions is a six bedroom mansion in Byron Bay, Australia. The mansion offers luxuries like a gym, steam room, spa, games room, media room, and other cool things.

Chris Hemsworth’s family – brothers, wives and children

The Thor actor is married to Elsa Pataky. She is a Spanish model, actress and film producer best known for her role as Elena Neves in The Fast and the Furious franchise. Hemsworth and Elsa began dating in early 2010 after meeting through their representatives. Both are represented by the same management company ROAR.

They tied the knot at Christmas 2010. The couple have three children together. They are daughter India (born May 2012) and twin sons Tristan and Sasha (born March 2014). Hemsworth has publicly expressed his hope that his children won’t grow up spoiled just because they have rich and famous parents.

Chris Hemsworth comes from a family of five. Among them is mother Leonie, teacher, and father Craig, social worker. Hemsworth has an older brother named Luke and a younger brother named Liam. Luke and Liam are also famous actors. Luke began his acting career in 2001/2002 on TV Neighbors. He has appeared in other television series, both in Australia and Hollywood. One of these is the HBO series Westworld . Even the youngest Hemsworth, Liam, is not doing badly at all. He has appeared in many popular films such as The Hunger Games, Love and Honor, The Seamstress, and The Last Song .

Height and other body measurements

Chris Hemsworth is a combination of talent and good looks that has undoubtedly boosted his career. He is 1.9 m tall and weighs 91 kg. Other known body measurements include his chest measuring 48 inches and his waist measuring 33 inches.

In 2014, People Magazine named him the sexiest man alive. Past recipients include David Beckham, Dwayne Johnson, Blake Shelton, and Idris Elba, among others.
