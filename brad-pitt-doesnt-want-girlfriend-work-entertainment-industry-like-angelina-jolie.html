Brad Pitt Doesnt Want New Girlfriend To Work In Entertainment Industry Like Angelina Jolie

Highlights

  • Brad Pitt's successful relationship with Ines De Ramon is attributed to her not being in the entertainment industry like his ex-wife Angelina Jolie.
  • Ines and Brad have a strong connection due to their shared love for art and their mellow and artistic personalities.
  • Brad and Ines want to have children together, despite Brad already having six kids, and are serious about their relationship.

Brad Pitt seems to have found happiness again with his girlfriend Ines De Ramon. But it appears the actor believes his relationship is successful in part because Ines isn’t in the entertainment industry, unlike Brad’s ex-wife Angelina Jolie.

Ines, who was previously married to Vampire Diaries actor Paul Wesley, works in the jewelry industry. As per PEOPLE, she’s currently the head of wholesale for Anita Ko jewelry, an LA-based designer that’s created pieces for celebs like Kourtney Kardashian and Hailey Bieber.

Though Ines may be used to rubbing shoulders with the rich and famous, having a life outside of the spotlight has helped her relationship with Brad blossom, a new report claims.

Brad’s Relationship With Ines Is Easier Since She Isn’t A Celebrity

“Brad and Ines have a nice, easy-going relationship because their personalities gel,” a source said. “They’re both mellow and artistic, and they feed on each other’s creativity. They both share a love of art.”

“He appreciates that Ines isn’t in the industry,” they continued. “And she seems totally unfazed by his celebrity.”

“Ines has been a breath of fresh air for Brad. She’s smart and down-to-earth and supportive,” the source went on. “It’s been a crazy year for Brad, but Ines is the one great thing that’s happened to him. Having her in his life has made it less stressful for sure.”

In contrast, Brad’s past marriages have been extremely high profile, and it’s hard not to assume the publicity took a toll on the relationships. He was married to Jennifer Aniston from 2000 to 2005, but got into a relationship with Angelina before their divorce was even finalized. This created a perceived love triangle in the tabloids that still captures the public’s attention to this day.

Brad And Ines Want To Start A Family Of Their Own

Even though Brad’s romantic life may be largely out of the spotlight, his relationship with his six kids continues to be a big source of speculation. Things are reportedly tense between Brad and his kids, and have been since an incident on a private plane led Angelina to file for divorce.

Reports say that not only is Brad focused on repairing things with his kids, but he’s hopeful about getting a second chance at fatherhood, as he and Ines reportedly want babies together.

“Brad and Ines are smitten with each other and she recently moved into his Los Feliz mansion, proving how serious they are,” a source spilled to Closer Magazine. “He’s known from the outset that Ines wants to become a mum and that’s something they discussed very early on, given their 30-year age gap.”

The insider said Ines was initially “surprised” that Brad is open to more kids, considering he’s already raised six, but he’s apparently insistent that it’s something he wants to do again.

“After everything he’s been through with his children, he’s keen to make a fresh start and create new memories,” the insider shared. “Brad’s not getting any younger, so it’s something they would like sooner rather than later. He’s even joked about doing the school run in his 70s and hopes that he’ll still be seen as a heartthrob!"

For now, Brad hasn’t officially commented on the speculation regarding his future family plans.
