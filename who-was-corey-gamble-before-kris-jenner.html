Corey Gamble Might Be Kris Jenner's Boyfriend, But That's Not His Most Lucrative Gig

Corey Gamble has managed to be a mystery despite spending years dating Kris Jenner and making appearances on multiple Keeping Up with the Kardashians episodes. He and his partner Kris sprung up a relationship shortly after the latter’s end of her 20-year marriage to Caitlyn Jenner. His relationship with the Kardashian momager has been pretty low-key, compared to Kris’ previous affairs.

While he prefers to keep his private life away from the public, many fans wonder who he really was before being connected to the Jenner-Kardashian family. Some people accuse him of dating Kris only for fame and her money. Others think that he’s actually working for her. Little do they know, he’s actually worth a pretty penny on his own. Here’s everything to know about Corey Gamble before becoming famous as Jenner’s beau.

Who Was Corey Gamble Before Kris Jenner?

Hailing from Atlanta, Georgia, Corey attended Westlake High School, where he was a member of the football team. Due to his ability to balance his schoolwork and sports activities, his friends called him “Mr. Dependable.”

After graduating in 1999, he went to Morehouse College where he completed his degree in business marketing.

Corey relocated to Los Angeles, California, after receiving his degree to pursue a career in the music industry.

Before becoming well-known as Jenner's lover, he had already established himself in the entertainment and marketing industry as a corporate executive and talent manager. He presently works for Scooter Braun's business, SB Projects, and thus served as Bieber's tour manager.

Clearly, working with Justin Bieber has earned Corey quite a bit of cash; he's actually a millionaire and has made more money in the past few years than he did in his entire career pre-2020.

Corey Gamble Is A Millionaire

As for his wealth, it proves that he works just as hard as Kris. Corey became a millionaire thanks to his work as a talent manager. Aside from working with Justin, the powerhouse agent also represented music stars such as Ariana Grande and Demi Lovato.

In addition, he began appearing on the Kardashian-Jenner family’s reality show in 2015. His net worth sits at a hefty $15 million.

In addition to being friends with Bieber, the talent manager has a number of well-known pals including Carl Lentz, the pastor of Hillsong, and hospitality tycoon David Grutman. A picture of him with fashion designer Tommy Hilfiger smoking cigars on a yacht was posted by him in 2017 on Instagram.

But among the celebrities he’s connected with, his infamous relationship with Kris Jenner has made his name more recognizable among the public.

Corey Gamble’s Relationship With Kris Jenner Has Defied The Odds

At a birthday celebration for fashion designer Riccardo Tisci's 40th birthday hosted by Kim's ex-boyfriend Kanye West in Ibiza in August 2014, Corey and Kris first came into contact. Soon after the occasion, Corey began to appear in Kris' photos, and the two have pretty much been inseparable ever since.

Rumor has it that Corey dated Sheree Buchanan, who starred on Atlanta Exes, for three years before crossing path with Kris. At the time the pair met, Kris was divorcing Caitlyn Jenner and was in the middle of the process.

It is unclear when the two made things official after meeting, but by October 2014, Gamble had joined the Kardashian family on a trip to Las Vegas for Kim’s birthday, so it is thought that Corey and Kris were dating by that point.

Fast-forward to 2015, the couple made their relationship Instagram-official while on a double date with Tish and Billy Ray Cyrus. Despite the 25-year age difference (Corey, who’s the same age as Kris’ second-eldest daughter Kim Kardashian, has even been called a gold digger) and some minor breakup rumors, Kris revealed in a 2021 interview that they are still going strong.

When asked about their relationship, she said that Corey was an incredible support system who was loved by the rest of her family.

He’s the greatest guy, and he’s just an amazing support system for me, and he really gives me a lot of strength and insight. He’s a great sounding board. And he loves my kids and my mom, and they love him.

As somewhat a response to the rumors, Corey posted a sweet birthday message for Kris. Celebrating Kris’ 66th birthday, the talent manager took to social media to share a bunch of photos from their years of togetherness.

He wrote, “Happppppy Birthday my Beautiful Queen!! Love you Deep bab. Cheers To the best mother & more decades of love and good health with u @krisjenner…”

The couple has been together since 2014 after meeting at a party. Since then, they have been open with their admiration of each other on social media, with Corey becoming a key family member.

Also, he has since been known to live a high-profile life just like the Kardashian-Jenner family. He is mostly seen in the events the family attends and is almost always present on their lavish trips.
