Is it pronounced Danganronpa or Danganronpa?

There’s two versions, both from the games. Day-n-gahn-rohn-puh, as pronounced by the robotic voice, and Dahn-gahn-ron-puh, as pronounced by Monokuma.

What does Danganronpa mean in English?

The phrase “Danganronpa” directly translates to “Bullet Refutation,” meaning “bullet” in the literal meaning and “refuting” in terms of refuting statements in the context of a trial.
