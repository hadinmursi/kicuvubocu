Bethany Mota Biography And Net Worth

Bethany Mota is an American fashion designer, singer, and video blogger on the YouTube channel Macbarbie07 which she created in 2009.

Bethany Mota who goes by the full name Bethany Noel Mota was born on November 7, 1995, in Merced County, California in the United States.

She rose to fame for her haul videos in which she shows her fashion and style purchases via the internet. She uploads videos of outfit ideas, makeup and hair tutorials, recipes, and more do-it-yourself ideas.

Mota who is of Mexican and Portuguese descent is the daughter of Tammy and Tony Mota.  She has her own fashion line called Aéropostale.

Mota has a sister, Brittany. Both Mota and Brittany grew up in Los Banos, California.

Her YouTube channel has garnered 9.8 million subscribers and over 900 million views.

Bethany Mota Net Worth

The multi-talented video blogger has acquired some wealth for herself mainly through YouTube and her clothing line. Bethany Mota has an estimated net worth of $3 million.
