Valerie C. Robinson Career

Robinson initially made her screen debut with a small role in the 1977’s TV movie Having Babies II as Terri. Additionally, in 1982, she appeared in another TV movie One Shoe Makes It Murder. In addition, Robinson portrayed the role of Fashion Center Beauty in ‘Over the Brooklyn Bridge.

Furthermore, Robinson has also appeared in the TV series ‘Lottery!’. She was cast in the movie ‘Patty Hearst’ for a small role. In 2018, she appeared as Frances Ledbetter in ‘Awful’. She is also recognized as Valerie Carpenter Bernstein.
