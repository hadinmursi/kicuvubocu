Hope Dworaczyk Smith, Husband Robert Smith Expecting Second Child

Round two! Model, healthy living advocate, and fashion editor Hope Dworaczyk Smith and her husband, investor Robert Smith, are expecting baby No. 2 following their summer wedding, Us Weekly can exclusively report. A rep for the couple confirms to Us they’re due this spring.

Robert, the founder, chairman, and CEO of the investment firm Vista Equity Partners, 52, and Hope, 30, tied the knot in a lavish July 2015 wedding and are already proud parents to son Hendrix, 10 months. 

In an adorable photo supplied to Us, Hendrix helps his parents make their big announcement by sitting before a to-do list, which includes “Become a big brother,” among other tasks.

Hope gave birth to her adorable little boy at home in December 2014. “I can’t believe I did that,” Hope told Us following Hendrix’s arrival. “It’a all a big blur right now. Kinda trippy to say the least!”
