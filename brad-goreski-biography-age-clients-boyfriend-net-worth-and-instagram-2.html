Brad Goreski Biography

Brad Goreski is a Canadian celebrity fashion stylist and television personality born on August 15, 1977. He attended the University of Southern California where he graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Art History.

During breaks from school, he worked as an intern for W and Vogue Magazines, and was subsequently hired as an assistant in the West Coast office of Vogue. He is best known for his dapper way of dressing, often including bow ties and bright suits within his daywear. Some of his clients include Jessica Alba, Demi Moore, Christina Ricci, Rashida Jones, Kaley Cuoco and Minka Kelly.

He met stylist Rachel Zoe and in 2008 he began working for her, and he became a part of a reality series “The Rachel Zoe Project” which is about her work and life. He then became the Style Director of her company. After leaving Rachel Zoe’s project, the spin-off series called “It’s a Brad, Brad World” was released and it featured his attempts to launch his own stylist business.

In 2012, he released his memoir, Born to be Brad, and was also named the exclusive brand stylist for Kate Spade NY. He also contributes editorial work to various magazines, including InStyle, Details, BlackBook, and LA Confidential.

In 2014, Goreski was named a new co-host of Fashion Police on the E! Network. In 2015, he joined Fashion Police as a new co-host.

Brad Goreski Age

He was born on 15 August 1977 in Port Perry, Canada. He is 41 years old as of 2018.

Brad Goreski and Gary Janetti

Brad is married to Gary Janetti whom he had dated for 16 years. The couple tied knot aboard a luxury cruise ship in the Caribbean on December 26.

Brad Goreski has an estimated net worth of $7 million. He owns a $5.6 million mansion in Beverly Hills which he bought in 2017 together with Gary Janetti.
