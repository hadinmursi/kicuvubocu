Find Nootrobox (HVMN) Net Worth/Revenue

The revenue/net worth of Nootrobox in 2021 has been a matter of curiosity among the public who followed the company year back in the show ‘Shark Tank.’

Nootrobox is an American company headquartered in San Francisco that manufactures and sells ketone ester drinks and products. 

Geoffrey Woo and Michael Brandt founded the company in the year 2014. Later in the year 2015, the Silicon Valley angel investors invested in the company worth $500000.

After collaborating with 7-Eleven locations in San Fransico, Nootrobox changed its name and made it H.V.M.N. (Health Via Modern Nutrition).

Similarly, in the year 2018, H.V.M.N. products affiliated with the United States Special Operations Command were the tested part of Operation Tech Warrior.
