Rachel Zoe Gives Birth, Welcomes Second Baby With Husband

OMG, she's a mom of two! Rachel Zoe and husband Rodger Berman welcomed their second child together she announced via Twitter on Sunday, Dec. 22.

"So excited to welcome our baby boy into the world…he's 7 lbs 12 oz, beautiful, healthy and we couldn't be happier," she shared with fans.

The 42-year-old fashion designer and Berman, who married in 1996, are already parents to son Skyler, 2.

After Us Weekly exclusively revealed in June that Zoe was expecting her second child, the celebrity stylist confirmed the pregnancy news in a blog post in September. "We are beyond excited for Skyler to have a sibling and for us to fall in love all over again," she wrote. "We feel incredibly blessed."
