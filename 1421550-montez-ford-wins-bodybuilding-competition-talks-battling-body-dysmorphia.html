Montez Ford Wins Bodybuilding Competition, Talks Battling Body Dysmorphia

WWE star Montez Ford challenged himself by competing in a bodybuilding competition.

On November 13, Montez Ford announced that he won his first bodybuilding competition. In an Instagram post, he stated that he took home first place and won his Pro Card.

Alongside the announcement, Ford candidly wrote about how he has been battling with body dysmorphia. He noted that he decided to challenge himself by training for a bodybuilding competition. Ford made it clear that he was thankful for the opportunity and for everyone’s support.

In recent weeks, Ford and his tag team partner, Angelo Dawkins, have been aligned with Bobby Lashley on WWE SmackDown.
