CMA Awards 2018 Red Carpet Fashion: See Celeb Dresses, Gowns

Country music’s biggest night has arrived! The 2018 Country Music Association Awards were held at the Bridgestone Arena in Nashville, Tennessee, on Wednesday, November 14, and Carrie Underwood and Brad Paisley were back for the 11th (!) consecutive year to co-host the show. And while we knew the pregnant emcee would provide plenty of chic maternity fashion inspo during the show, we are equally excited to see what all the other celebrities were wearing on the CMAs red carpet.

Miranda Lambert, Kacey Musgraves, Bebe Rexha, Maren Morris and Lauren Alaina were just a few of the stylish country stars nominated and slated to perform, and, as last year’s glitzy red carpet indicated, we were in for all kinds of sparkle and shine.
