High School Musicals Corbin Bleu Marries Sasha Clements

A Wildcat wedding! High School Musical star Corbin Bleu and his fiancée, Sasha Clements, tied the knot on Saturday, July 23, the actor’s rep confirms to Us Weekly.

As Us Weekly previously reported, Bleu, 27, and Clements, 26, got engaged in front of Cinderella’s Castle at Walt Disney World in Orlando, Florida, in October 2014. The Disney alum proposed to his longtime girlfriend with a stunning engagement ring that was placed inside of a glass slipper.

The pair first met in September 2011 at a grocery store in Toronto. Days before they tied the knot, Clements, who is best known for her role in Teletoon’s Majority Rules!, celebrated with a bachelorette party at the Malibu Wine Safaris.

Bleu took to Instagram shortly after the bachelorette celebration to poke fun at his future bride, sharing a photo of the actress and a giraffe. “Right before [a giraffe] tried to get to 1st base with my future wifey,” he captioned the shot. “Thank you @malibusafaris for making her day perfect! #sashasbachelorette.”
