Justina Machado Biography

Justina Machado (b. Justina Milagros Machado Sept 6, 1972) is an American actress who was born and brought up in Chicago, IL, United States of America. Machado is famous for her role as Vanessa Diaz on the HBO drama series Six Feet Under and Penelope Alvarez on One Day at a Time. Additionally, she is widely popular for her role as Darci Factor in the CW show Jane the Virgin and Brenda on the drama Queen of the South.
