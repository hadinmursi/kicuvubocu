Philip Michael Thomas Net Worth

As the detective, Ricardo Tubbs on the popular TV show Miami Vice from the 1980s, Philip Michael Thomas, an American actor, and musician, is most recognized for this character.

His first major performances were in the 1976 picture Sparkle, starring Irene Cara.
Following his breakthrough in Miami Vice, he made multiple appearances in made-for-TV films and commercials for phone psychic services.

He was a spokesperson for the cell phone entertainment firm Nextones, and he also voiced the character Lance Vance in the computer games Grand Theft Auto: Vice City (2002) and Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories (2006).

Philip Michael Thomas Net Worth

Philip Michael Thomas is a $2.5 million net-worth American actor. He is best known for his portrayal of detective Ricardo Tubbs (“Tubbs”) in the hit 1980s TV show Miami Vice.

He has received a People’s Choice Award as well as a Golden Globe nomination. Thomas is credited with coining the acronym “EGOT,” which stands for Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, and Tony.

He intended to win all four one day, but as of this writing, he has yet to do so.
