Who Is Miles Teller?

Miles Teller is an American actor well known for starring in the 2014 film Whiplash, as a jazz drummer boy and in Top Gun: Maverick (2022), as a pilot alongside Tom Cruise.

Born on February 20, 1987, Teller grew up in the neighborhoods of  Pennsylvania and Delaware.

He picked interest in acting and music as a high school teen, and joined his school’s drama club. He also learnt how to play the alto saxophone, drums, piano, and guitar.

Teller attendedLecanto High School in Lecanto, Florida; and bagged a degree from the New York University Tisch School of the Arts, at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute and screen acting.

Upon graduation, he began appearing in short films and made his acting debut with the movie Rabbit Hole in 2010.

Miles Teller Career

Teller began to move up the ladder of fame after appearing in the 2013 film The Spectacular Now.

The following year, he stared in the 2014 Academy Award-winning drama Whiplash, and got his breakthrough.

He went on to star in other movies, including the superhero film Fantastic Four, War Dogs, Bleed for This, Top Gun: Maverick, and Only the Brave, Spiderhead.

Other movies where he appeared include the Amazon crime drama Too Old to Die Young, The Offer and The God Father.
