Aviva Drescher Throws Prosthetic Leg During RHONY FIght

Table-flipping? Passe. Wig-snatching? Over! Wineglass-tossing? Please! As promised and teased for what seemed like years, Real Housewives of New York delivered the most outlandish and epic of catfight stunts in the history of the franchise: The fake leg throw. For the season six finale which aired on Tuesday, July 22, erratic, frequently absent housewife Aviva Drescher used a very personal prop—her artificial leg—to make a point as she bickered with her costars in the middle of Le Cirque.

Drescher was a bit of a surprise guest at Le Cirque, where Sonja Morgan was hosting a “Team Sonja” fete to acknowledge her entourage of supporters. Drescher, as fans know, had flaked on countless outings and excursions throughout the season, most notably a girls’ trip to the Berkshires and, more recently, a Kristen Taekman-coordinated jaunt to Montana. When Drescher suddenly claimed an asthma affliction prevented her from joining the ladies on the cowgirl jaunt, just about everyone smelled BS.

At Le Cirque, Heather Thomson was quick to call her out. “This is more than asthma,” she said. When the women piled on—doubting the doctor’s X-ray Drescher had produced as proof—Ramona Singer offered, “I think it’s because you didn’t want to leave your husband.”

The sniping escalated, and Drescher dredged up an old battle with Carole Radziwill regarding the authorship of her books.

When Thomson said it was “laughable” that Drescher “never lied” to the women, that apparently was the last straw.

“The only thing artificial or fake about me is this!” Drescher yelled as she thwonked her fake leg on the table. “Everything else is real! I’m sick of being called fake!” Next, she picked up the expensive, manicured, well-heeled fake leg and threw it across the room and the women and other bystanders looked on in absolute shock.
