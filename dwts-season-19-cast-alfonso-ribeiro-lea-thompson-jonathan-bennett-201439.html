DWTS Season 19 Cast: Alfonso Ribeiro, Lea Thompson, Jonathan Bennett

So fetch! The latest batch of celebs set to compete in season 19 of Dancing With the Stars include Mean Girls' Jonathan Bennett, Back To the Future actress Lea Thompson, YouTube star Bethany Mota and The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air's Alfonso Ribeiro, Us Weekly can confirm.

The four additions join previously reported contestants Janel Parrish (Pretty Little Liars), Olympian Lolo Jones, and soap opera hunk Antonio Sabato Jr. 

The full list of competitors in hopes of winning the coveted Mirrorball Trophy will be revealed live on Good Morning America on Thursday, Sept. 4, less than two weeks before the season 19 premiere on Monday, Sept. 15.

The group will be dancing with previously announced pros including fan favorites Mark Ballas, Cheryl Burke, Valentin Chmerkovskiy, Derek Hough, Tony Dovolani, Peta Murgatroyd, and Karina Smirnoff.

DWTS vet Julianne Hough is also making a comeback. The Safe Haven actress is set to complete the judges panel alongside Len Goodman, Carrie Ann Inaba and Bruno Tonioli. "I'm so excited to be back with my Dancing With the Stars family again on a regular basis," she said in a press release.
