Nathan Purdee Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Nathan Purdee (born August 6, 1950 in Tampa, Florida) is an American serial actor who has appeared in a number of films. Due to the fact that Purdee’s father was a United States Air Force officer, the family was forced to relocate many times. In Denver, Colorado, he received his high school diploma after spending his formative years in cities such as Washington, D.C.; Bakersfield, California; and Kaiserslautern, Germany. He currently resides in the Pennsylvania town of Easton. On the CBS serial opera The Young and the Restless, he first gained widespread attention as mobster-turned-private detective Nathan Hastings, whom he played from 1985 until 1992. His appearance on Y&R (where he was one of just two black cast members on the programme at its heyday in the late 1980s) opened the way for the subsequent inclusion of additional African American actors to the cast. Having departed Young and the Restless in December 1991, he relocated to New York in January 1992 to take on the role of District Attorney Hank Gannon on the ABC serial opera One Life to Live, a position he would hold until his death in 2003. On February 11, 2009, it was revealed that he will be returning to One Life to Live in April of the same year as his departure.
