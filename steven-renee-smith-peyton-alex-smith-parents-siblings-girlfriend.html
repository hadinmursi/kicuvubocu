Steven &amp; Renee Smith Peyton Alex Smith Parents Siblings &amp; Girlfriend

Steven and Renee Smith are the parents of Peyton Alex Smith. Peyton is an American actor known for his role in the 2022 TV series All American: Homecoming.

Apart from that, he has also appeared in other movies including  Legacies (2018-2021), The Quad (2017-2018), Detroit (2017), Carter High (2015), Mad Money (2008), and more. While he has established himself as an actor, he has also pursued a career in music.

Smith has given his vocals to the Quad’s theme song, Rise Up Together. He prominently rose to fame when he appeared on Homecoming. Producer Ed Tapia cast him for the role of Damond Sims alongside Geffri Maya, Sylvester Powell, Cory Hardrict, Mitchell Edwards, Kelly Jenrette, Netta Walker, and Camille Hyde.

He is also well-known for his role as Rafael Waithe in “Legacies.” Waithe is a young werewolf who unwittingly activated his curse after he mistakenly murdered his girlfriend, Cassie.

Smith has shared the screen with celebrities like Da’Vinchi, Eric Kofi-Abrefa, and Lil Meech. While he is growing to fame over the years, people have been getting more curious about his personal life.

Meet Peyton Alex Smith Parents Steven and Renee Smith: His Siblings

Peyton Alex Smith was born to his parents Steven Smith and Renee Jackson Smith in San Diego, California. He grew up with his sibling Patrick Smith in Texas where he currently resides.

Mr. and Mrs. Smith welcomed Peyton on 18 June 1994. He went to Dr. John D. Horn High School to finish his high school education.

Later on, he went to Florida A&M University for his advanced-level studies. However, dropped out after the first semester and started focusing on acting.

He then went to Booker T. Washington High School for the Performing and Visual Arts for his acting skills. Both Steven and Renee have always stayed supportive of their son’s career.
