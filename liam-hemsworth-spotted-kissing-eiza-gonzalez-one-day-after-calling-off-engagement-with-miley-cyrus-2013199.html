Liam Hemsworth, Eiza Gonzalez Kiss One Day After Miley Cyrus Split

Liam Hemsworth is moving on! One day after Miley Cyrus' rep confirmed that the couple have called off their engagement, the Hunger Games actor was photographed hooking up with Mexican actress and singer Eiza Gonzalez in Los Angeles.

Dressed in workout clothes and a Yankees baseball hat, the 23-year-old actor was caught making out with Gonzalez in a parking garage after running errands together on Tuesday, Sept. 17. The Aussie actor wrapped his arms around her as they passionately kissed. Newly-single herself, Gonzalez (who previously dated Mexican businessman Pepe Diaz for two years before splitting over the summer) showed off her tiny frame in short shorts and a sports bra during the intimate moment.

The Catching Fire star moved fast with his new fling after briefly meeting for the first time three days earlier. After stepping out solo at the Floyd Mayweather Jr. vs. Canelo Alvarez fight in Las Vegas, the heartthrob was seen flirting with Gonzalez alongside a group of friends at XS nightclub at the Encore hotel. 

It has been quite the week for the blonde hunk, whose relationship with the "Wrecking Ball" singer has been anything but stable for the past few months. As a source previously revealed to Us Weekly, the pair was just "weeks away from calling it off permanently." Another source added: "He is over her and wants to make a clean break. No more back and forth."
